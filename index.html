How It Works

Four Steps to a Perfect Prompt

No guessing, no blank cursor. Just pick your options and get a prompt built for exactly how you learn.

01
🎓
Pick Your Grade & Subject

Select your grade (6th–12th) and one of 12 subject areas. Your skill level for that specific subject appears right below — because you might be advanced in history but struggling in math.

02
📚
Choose a Topic

Pick from 16 specific topics per subject — everything from Quadratic Equations to the Civil Rights Movement to Beat Making. Add optional context like your chapter number or test date for an even sharper prompt.

03
⚡
Select a Learning Activity

Tell the AI HOW to help you. Choose from 14 activities: Explain It, Quiz Me, Study Guide, Practice Problems, Flashcard Set, Exam Review, Vocabulary Builder, and more.

04
📋
Copy & Paste Anywhere

Your custom prompt is generated instantly. One click copies it — paste into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any free AI chatbot and get expert tutoring in seconds.
